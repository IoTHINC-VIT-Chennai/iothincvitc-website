OUR AIM

Our aim is to create a IoT Enthusiast community and encourage students to become aware about the technology, IoT related sectors, build projects, conduct events, workshops, etc. This community would be motivating students of VIT Chennai to work on innovative hardware and software based projects. With the plethora of interdiscplinary departments of IoTHINC VITC, students and members of this club get to learn, build and innovate altogether at once.
